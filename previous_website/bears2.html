Brown bears

The brown bear or grizzly bear(Ursus arctos) is native to parts of northern
Eurasia and North America. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
There are many subspecies within the brown bear species, including the Atlas bear and the Himalayan
brown bear.Bears don't gather food for the winter instead they hibernate(Sleep over winter).
