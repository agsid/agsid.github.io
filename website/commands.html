Available Commands

These are the commands you can use to program your FLL bot. Distances are in inches, angles in degrees, and time in seconds.

Block Action Example
Move the Robot Move forward or backward by a certain distance. drive(12)
Turn the Robot Turn the robot clockwise or counter-clockwise. turn(90)
Control the Arm Move the arm up, down, or reset its position. arm("up")
Display a message Show a message in the console. print("Hello World")
Wait Pause the program for a specific number of seconds. wait(2)
