Happy Birthday

Dear Mumma,

Happy birthday! Thank you so much for always supporting me. I have been seeing all my friends and have appreciated you so much more. You are always ready to put effort in, always ready to support me, and always there when I need you.

I have noticed whenever any big task needs to be done you are always ready to put the effort in. You are very hardworking, and have never limited your scope because of the work that was needed to pull any task off. Not only do you have brilliant planning, but you are able to pull off your plans by yourself. For example, recently you have completely transformed the house without adding a whole new bedroom. Even though it was such a big task you were ready to put the work in; the only days you were free, you finished the task.

Not only this, but whenever I need support from you, you are always in the stands with me cheering me on. I will remember this throughout my life. You make time in your schedule and come just to support me with what I do.

Thank you so much, and Happy Birthday! 🎉

- Sid Agrawal 2026
