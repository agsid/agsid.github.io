Robot Commands

drive

Moves the robot forward or backward by a set distance.

drive(24)

turn

Rotates the robot by a given angle in degrees.

turn(90)

speed

Sets the robot’s movement speed (0–100).

speed(80)

wait

Pauses movement for a specific duration.

wait(2)

arm_speed

Controls the speed of the robot’s arm motor.

arm_speed(60)

arm

Moves the arm up or down by a degree value.

arm(-45)
